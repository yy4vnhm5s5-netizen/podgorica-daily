# Vision

Podgorica Daily is a trusted, fast, and accessible guide to daily life in Podgorica. It makes local information easier to find without hiding its source, freshness, or limitations.

## Principles

- **Local utility first:** solve repeatable local needs.
- **Trust is visible:** show source, timestamp, attribution, and status where relevant.
- **Accessible by default:** support small screens, keyboards, assistive technology, and constrained networks.
- **Privacy by design:** collect only data needed for a clear purpose.
- **Maintainable growth:** evolve providers and features behind stable boundaries.

Sprint 0 defines standards and delivery foundations only. Future weather, mobility, events, search, maps, editorial operations, and AI summaries must preserve these principles.
